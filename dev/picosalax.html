Picosalax


This video will help demonstrate how to prepare your Picosalax medication. Picosalax is taken in 2 doses or what has been prescribed to you by your doctor. The timing of those doses should have been on a printout provided to you by your doctor. Bowel Prep text messages will be sent to help remember when to take your doses. Drink the solution slowly but completely. Please continue drinking adequate clear fluids. Refer to the Consumer Medical Information in the link above for the full medication details.
